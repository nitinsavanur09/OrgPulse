Section 3 · Agentforce running cost New in V2
What Agentforce will cost to run.
Based on your actual case volume of 2,840 cases/month across your top 5 case reason categories. Three deflection scenarios — conservative, likely, and optimistic — at current Salesforce Flex Credit list pricing.
How to read this — and why the economics are strongly positive
Agentforce charges per conversation turn via Flex Credits. Each deflected tier-1 case consumes approximately 4–5 Credits at $0.10 each — a total cost of $0.40–$0.50 per deflected case. Your current average handling cost per case (staff time, tooling) is approximately $18. Even at the conservative 30% deflection scenario, the net saving per deflected case is over $17. The Flex Credit cost in every scenario above is a fraction of the value delivered.
